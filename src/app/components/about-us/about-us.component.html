About Us

Miranda guest house is a stunning venue and conference centre tucked away in the valley below the majestic Qwaqwa Mountains.
Just 15 minutes drive to natural attractions such as Golden Gate and Sterkfontein Dam, and 45 minutes from the
beautiful town of Clarens, Miranda caters for business and pleasure. Our team of expert event planners can help you coordinate
the perfect function: from weddings to conferences. Our comfortable, affordable accommodation suits the business person, the intrepid
explorer and the family getaway.
